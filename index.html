F.A.Q.

About Nahi's Tea

We are a team specialized in blending teas. On the market since August, 2022, we work hard everyday to imagine a new tea time experience and we test the results with a large number of professionals and volunteers. Our goal is to amaze your palate with new favlors, textures and please your soul with the fragrances and tastes.

How to subscribe?

To subscribe, go to our "Pricing" section and choose a plan. After that, you'll be redirected to a payment page, where you'll insert your payment method, address and billing adress. We accept all flags of credit and debt cards.

How to check my Tea box's status?

After you placed your order and received a confirmation email, you should receive another email with the tracking code. You can also see the tracking code in our site, under the the "My Subscriptions" tab. You can click the code to pop up the details of the tracking or copy and paste it on your local delivery service's site.

How does payment works through months?

When you subscribe, the first payment will occur instantly, and it will be billed on the day of the subscribe. Then, for all the next active months of the subscription, the billing will occur on the 15th day of that month.

Cancellation conditions

On the Monthly Plan, you can unsubscribe at any moment after receiving your box, with no additional fees or fidelity terms. In other plans, you aggree to a fidelity term, so when you cancel before the end of the term, you'll pay a fine corresponding to 50% of the remaining ammount. The cancellation should be done through the email "contact@nahi-tea.com" with the topic "SUBSCRIPTION CANCELLATION", and our team will answer within 24 hours.

How should I prepare my tea?

In every Tea Box, the teas will accompany some instruction on how to prepare each one of them. You can follow the instructions and opt to drink it hot or cold. Certify of the quantity of tea needed, water's temperature and adequated infusion time.
